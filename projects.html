Law helper

2024

Law helper is a web app created in Flask which helps users to resolve doubts connected with law. It can help them with criminal or inheritance cases. In the criminal section they can write in a form an action they did and the app shows the proper article for this. In the inheritance section they can write some problems connected with inheritance and the app shows the solution. If the description given by user is not accurate, the app gives the subsidiary form with additional questions in order to specify the description.
